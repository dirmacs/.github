# Contributing to DIRMACS

Thank you for your interest in contributing to DIRMACS projects.

- **Repositories:** Each project (ares, thulp, daedra, doltares, etc.) may have its own contributing guidelines in that repo's `CONTRIBUTING.md`. Check the specific repository first.
- **General:** We welcome issues, pull requests, and discussion. Open an issue or PR in the relevant repository, or reach out at [contact@dirmacs.com](mailto:contact@dirmacs.com).
- **Code of conduct:** Be respectful and constructive. See [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) for our community standards.
